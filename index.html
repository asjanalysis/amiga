AMIGA STARFIRE

Blast through the Chromatic Belt, collect weapon crystals, and survive the drone fleets.

Keyboard: arrows/WASD move, Space/Z fire, P pause, M mute. Touch: drag ship to fly.

Mission

Defend the orbital lanes from crystalline raiders and biomech drones.

Power-ups

W upgrades spread fire, S restores shields, L grants an extra life.

Style

Low-resolution canvas, chunky sprites, dithering, bright palettes, and punchy synth bleeps.
